Morant was a three-star recruit in high school

Morant Fun Fact 3 min read

Ja Morant is one of the league’s most explosive point guards.

He was rated a three-star recruit in high school before a stunning rise.

Known for playmaking and clutch scoring, he was once underrated.

That evaluation reflected early talent and untapped potential.

Few three-star prospects become superstars; Morant is a notable exception.

It also shows the limits of rating systems and the power of development.

Morant embodies exceeding expectations through work and growth.
