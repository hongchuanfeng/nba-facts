Porzingis’s brother was a professional basketball player

Porzingis Fun Fact 3 min read

Kristaps Porziņģis’s brother, Jānis, played professionally across Europe and often advised and trained with him.

Latvian hoops prizes skill, spacing, and touch; Jānis’s path and network helped Kristaps join Sevilla’s youth setup early.

They emphasize off-season work on strength, footwork efficiency, and high-post shooting—blending “tall shooter” and “spacey rim protector” traits in KP.

Jānis’s experience also informed agency, medical, and contract decisions, aiding KP’s rehab and load management.

For many European prospects, family insiders provide resources and a buffer for cultural adjustment, league pace, and media pressure.
