About

Learn the story, vision, and team behind NBA Fun Facts

Our Mission

NBA Fun Facts is dedicated to discovering, organizing, and sharing lesser‑known yet reliable and valuable NBA knowledge—past and present. We systematize scattered information into readable, searchable, and extensible content.

We stand for accuracy, clear structure, and great experience. This means professional sourcing and thoughtful presentation that remain friendly and sustainable.

Who We Are

We are creators and developers who love basketball and knowledge sharing. We focus on player stories, team history, rules evolution, stats trivia, and cultural context—showcasing the NBA’s multidimensional beauty.

How We Work

We cross‑verify with public and trustworthy sources; use scripts to ensure consistent, maintainable formats; and pursue clear, readable, and extensible design and interactions.
