1980 Finals: A Player Earned 26 Free Throws in One Game

1980 Finals Fun Fact 3 min read

In the 1980 NBA Finals, one player attempted 26 free throws in a game.

Finals intensity often produces unusual stat lines—this one stood out.

It reflected aggressive play, officiating, and relentless rim pressure.

Few Finals games see so many trips to the line by a single player.

It’s a reminder of how free throws can swing championship moments.
