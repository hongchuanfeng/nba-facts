Lillard was rated a three-star recruit in high school

Lillard Fun Fact 3 min read

As one of the NBA’s top point guards, Damian Lillard’s career is legendary.

This fun fact highlights that Lillard was once rated a three-star recruit in high school before his remarkable rise.

Known for clutch scoring and leadership, few realize he was evaluated as a three-star prospect as a teenager.

That seemingly modest rating reflected early talent and potential yet to be fully realized.

Few players climb from three-star status to superstardom; Lillard’s ascent is one of the most notable.

The story sheds light on his early journey and the complexity of scouting evaluations.

In NBA history, Lillard is both a great player and an inspiring overachiever.
