The first NBA season with the three-point line

Historic first Fun Fact 3 min read

It marked a shift from a two-point era to today’s spacing-driven style.

The rule elevated strategy, entertainment, and competitive dynamics.

Few changes have had such lasting impact on NBA play.

Understanding this rule helps explain modern offense and floor spacing.

It remains a pivotal turning point in NBA evolution.
